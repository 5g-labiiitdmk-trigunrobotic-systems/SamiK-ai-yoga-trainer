SamiK AI Yoga Trainer

A Product of Trigun Robotics Private Limited, Kurnool. SamiK helps you practice yoga correctly at home by watching your pose through the camera, checking your accuracy in real time, and guiding you with voice feedback just like a personal yoga instructor.

What SamiK Does

  • Detects your body pose live using your device camera
  • Compares your posture with the correct yoga pose angles
  • Shows a real-time accuracy score for "Adjust Position"
  • Holds a timer once your pose is correct ("Hold Pose")
  • Gives spoken voice guidance and corrections in multiple Indian languages
  • Tracks your sessions, attendance, and performance over time

How to Use SamiK

  1. Register / Login – Create an account with your name, email, phone, age, gender and city, then log in. If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it with your registered email and phone number.
  2. Choose a category – From the welcome screen, pick a yoga category such as Suryanamaskaram, Meditation, Hatha, Yin, Kundalini or Vinyasa.
  3. Set your level – Open Settings (gear icon) and choose Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. This adjusts how strict the accuracy check is.
  4. Allow camera access – When prompted, allow the browser to use your camera so SamiK can see your pose.
  5. Position yourself – Stand where your full body is visible. The "Adjust Position" panel will show how closely you match the pose.
  6. Hold the pose – Once your accuracy is high enough, the "Hold Pose" timer starts automatically. Stay steady until it completes.
  7. Listen to feedback – SamiK speaks corrections (e.g. "straighten your back") to help you improve your pose.
  8. Take breaks – Between poses, a short break timer appears before the next pose begins.
  9. Finish the session – At the end, you'll see your average accuracy and total time for the session.
  10. Review progress – Check your Profile for attendance calendar and performance history.

Yoga Categories Overview

Suryanamaskaram: A sequence of 12 flowing poses that energizes the body, improves flexibility, and promotes overall fitness. It synchronizes movement with breathing for a complete workout.

Hatha Yoga: Focuses on traditional postures and controlled breathing techniques. It helps build strength, balance, concentration, and relaxation.

Yin Yoga: A slow-paced style where poses are held for longer durations. It improves joint mobility, deep flexibility, and mindfulness.

Vinyasa Yoga: A dynamic practice that links breath with movement in a continuous flow. It enhances stamina, coordination, and cardiovascular health.

Kundalini Yoga: Combines postures, breathing, meditation, and chanting to awaken inner energy. It supports mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual growth.

Settings & Customization

  • Difficulty Level – Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced accuracy thresholds
  • Voice Type – Choose between Male or Female voice guidance
  • Language – Pose names and instructions available in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Bengali, Gujarati, Malayalam, Marathi, Punjabi, Odia, Assamese, Nepali, Sanskrit, Kashmiri, Konkani and Urdu
  • Voice Volume – Adjust the volume of spoken feedback
Tip: Use the "Test Voice" button in Settings to preview how the voice guidance sounds before starting your session.

Privacy & Safety

Your camera feed is processed on your own device to detect your pose — it is used only to give you real-time feedback during your session. Please practice on a flat, non-slippery surface with enough open space around you, and consult a doctor before starting any new exercise routine if you have a medical condition.Your video is processed in real time and is not stored or recorded.
